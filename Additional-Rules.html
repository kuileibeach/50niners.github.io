O59 Summer League will follow the standard Ranger League rules except for the following exceptions: The following are some special Rules for the Rangers Over 59 Summer League Division

  • Click here to view O59 Summer League Format
    • No Slide Tackling– first time offense Yellow Card, second offense Red Card
    • Players receiving yellow cards must sit out 10 minutes. Team will be able to substitute the carded player
    • Over 67 players (Birth Year 1952) must be distinguished by wearing yellow shorts (or other contrasting color in the event that the team’s uniform shorts’ color is already yellow). Players over 67 may earn two (2) points when scoring from the field (only 1 point for penalty kicks), ONLY if wearing yellow shorts. Otherwise, goals will count as one (1) point. Exception, if an O67 player is fouled in the penalty area, O67 can take a 2pt penalty kick
    • The Disciplinary Committee is the Manager from each team. Each team has one vote. If there is a tie, the League President will have the deciding vote. Official protests must be submitted in writing within 24 hrs. of a game ending or after receiving official notice of a Ranger League disciplinary penalty.
    • Each team should field at least two O67 player at all times or otherwise play with less players. In order to help teams field a O67 player, O67 players will be allowed to play on any team if team does not have required number of O67 players.
    • If Team has less than (7 or 8) players, they may use league players from other teams to field 7 or 8) players. "Borrowed" Player's goals will not be counted as a score
    • Players aged 70 (Birth Year 1949) and over may play on any team to become the extra player on field.
    • Any players fouling an O67 player wearing Gold shorts will get an automatic yellow card and must sit out 10 minutes – team can substitute the yellow carded player.
