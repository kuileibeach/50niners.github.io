Do I need to be registered with the Hawaii Soccer Association (HSA)?
Yes, For insurance purposes. HSA fee is $25

How much are the player fees? Right now we are estimating the fee will be between $50 and $70.

Will the offsides rule be enforced? Yes.

Are referees giving cards for offenses? Yes. Red card suspensions will be decided by the Board.

How many games will each team play? Six regular season games and 2 playoffs. Total of 8 games for each team.
